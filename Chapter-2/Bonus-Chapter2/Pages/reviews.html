The Hunger Games

Reviews

IGN(8.5/10) says- “Jennifer Lawrence delivers a standout performance, making Katniss Everdeen an unforgettable heroine.”

Rotten Tomatoes: The Hunger Games holds an 84% critic score and an 81% audience score, praised for its thrilling action and strong performances.

Audience Reactions: Fans love the intense action, emotional depth and world-building, making it one of the best book-to-film adaptations.

The Guardian (4/5 stars): “A gripping and emotionally powerful adaptation that brings Suzanne Collins’ dystopian world to life.”

Awards: The film won Best Female Performance (MTV Movie Awards) and was nominated for Best Sci-Fi/Fantasy Film (Saturn Awards).
