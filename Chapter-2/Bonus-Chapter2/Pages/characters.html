The Hunger Games

Characters

Katniss Everdeen- A skilled and brave tribute from District 12 who volunteers for the Hunger Games and becomes a symbol of rebellion.

Peeta Mellark- A kind and strategic tribute who loves Katniss and plays the Games wisely.

Gale Hawthorne- Katniss's childhood best friend and a strong-willed fighter against the Capitol.

Haymitch Abernathy- A former Hunger Games victor from District 12 who becomes a mentor to Katniss and Peeta, known for his sharp wit and survival instincts.

Effie Trinket- The eccentric and colorful escort for District 12 tributes. She is known for her strict adherence to rules and Capitol fashion.

President Coriolanus Snow- The ruthless and manipulative leader of Panem, who uses the Hunger Games to maintain control over the districts.
